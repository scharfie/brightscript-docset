Getting Started

  • Created by Bill Reid, last modified on Oct 05, 2015
Go to start of metadata

The following sections will walk you through creating a developer account and linking your box to that account.

Software Updates

These instructions assume that you have an up-to-date version of the Roku Streaming Device firmware. Please make sure that your firmware is updated before starting this process.

Creating a Roku Account

Account creation is done at https://my.roku.com website. To get started, sign in to your Roku account or create a new Roku account.

 

Linking Your Roku Streaming Device

You must link your device to your Roku account to access the Channel Store. This process uses the rendezvous style of linking and involves getting the link code from your Roku streaming device and entering it into the link page on the website. The device will present the account linking page to the user when they go to Streaming Channels on the device for the first time.

Signing up for the Developer Program

In order to upload or publish a channel, you need to become a registered developer. Enroll in the Developer Program by selecting the Developer Site link on the Roku account page.

 

When you haven't yet registered as a Roku developer, you get the developer home page above. You may click on Enroll in Roku’s Developer Program.

After you enroll in Roku’s Developer Program, you can also enroll in Roku’s Billing Services Program to monetize your channel.

You can also enroll in Roku Billing at a later time, by clicking on Enroll in Roku Billing Services from your developer home.
