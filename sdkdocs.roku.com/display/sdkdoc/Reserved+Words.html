Reserved Words

Skip to end of metadata
  • Created by Bill Reid, last modified on Jan 27, 2016
Go to start of metadata

Like other programming languages, BrightScript has a number of reserved words that have specific meanings to the Roku platform.  These words cannot be used for other purposes in your channels. These words include:

  • And
  • Box
  • CreateObject
  • Dim
  • Each
  • Else
  • ElseIf
  • End
  • EndFunction
  • EndIf
  • EndSub
  • EndWhile
  • Eval
  • Exit
  • ExitWhile
